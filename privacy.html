Privacy Policy

This app (“Revspond”) processes content solely to assist in generating reply suggestions for customer reviews.

Entered text is transmitted temporarily to an external AI service exclusively for the purpose of generating a reply. The provider does not permanently store this content, does not perform tracking, and does not create user profiles.

No personal data is shared for advertising or analytics purposes.

The app processes only content actively provided or generated by the user:

  • review texts
  • optional names
  • generated reply suggestions
  • app settings (language, tone, signature)
  • subscription status
  • optional locally stored reply history

All locally stored content remains on the user’s device.

The app does not require special device permissions and does not use analytics or tracking services.

Generated texts are provided solely as writing assistance. Responsibility for reviewing, modifying, and publishing replies rests entirely with the user.
